# Denvan Holdings LLC -- Compliance Forms & Templates

This folder contains all compliance forms, templates, checklists, and worksheets needed to keep Denvan Holdings LLC compliant with Texas state law and federal regulations.

> **DISCLAIMER:** These templates are for informational and organizational purposes only. They do not constitute legal, tax, or financial advice. Consult with a qualified attorney, CPA, or tax professional before relying on any of these documents for legal or regulatory compliance.

---

## Folder Structure

### 01-Formation
Entity formation and registered agent documents.
- `certificate-of-formation-checklist.md` -- Checklist for Texas SOS Form 205 filing
- `registered-agent-designation.md` -- Template for designating/changing registered agent (Form 401)
- `certificate-of-fact-request.md` -- How to request a Certificate of Good Standing

### 02-Governance
Corporate governance documents and templates.
- `operating-agreement-template.md` -- Comprehensive LLC Operating Agreement template
- `member-meeting-minutes-template.md` -- Template for recording meeting minutes
- `written-consent-resolution-template.md` -- Resolutions in lieu of meeting (BOC 101.358)
- `membership-interest-certificate-template.md` -- Membership interest certificate template

### 03-Tax-Compliance
Texas and federal tax compliance checklists.
- `texas-franchise-tax-checklist.md` -- Texas Franchise Tax filing guide (due May 15 annually)
- `federal-tax-obligations-checklist.md` -- Federal tax obligations for LLCs
- `sales-tax-permit-checklist.md` -- Texas Sales & Use Tax compliance

### 04-Annual-Filings
Annual compliance tracking and preparation.
- `annual-compliance-calendar.md` -- Month-by-month deadline calendar
- `annual-report-preparation-worksheet.md` -- Worksheet for Public Information Report (Form 05-102)
- `compliance-audit-checklist.md` -- Internal compliance audit checklist

### 05-DBA-Subsidiaries
DBA registration and subsidiary setup.
- `assumed-name-certificate-checklist.md` -- Texas DBA filing guide (Form 503)
- `subsidiary-setup-checklist.md` -- Master checklist for launching a new subsidiary

### 06-Employment
Employment and contractor compliance.
- `new-hire-checklist.md` -- New hire compliance checklist (W-4, I-9, Texas new hire reporting)
- `independent-contractor-checklist.md` -- Contractor engagement and 1099 compliance

### 07-BOI-Report
Beneficial Ownership Information reporting (Corporate Transparency Act).
- `beneficial-ownership-information-report.md` -- BOI report preparation guide and worksheet
- `boi-change-tracking-log.md` -- Log for tracking changes that trigger BOI updates

## Critical Deadlines Summary

| Deadline | Filing | Agency |
|----------|--------|--------|
| January 31 | W-2s and 1099s to recipients | IRS |
| March 15 | Partnership / S-Corp tax returns (Form 1065/1120-S) | IRS |
| April 15 | Individual tax returns / Q1 estimated taxes | IRS |
| **May 15** | **Texas Franchise Tax Report + Public Information Report** | **Texas Comptroller** |
| June 15 | Q2 estimated taxes | IRS |
| September 15 | Q3 estimated taxes / Extended partnership returns | IRS |
| October 15 | Extended individual returns | IRS |
| Within 30 days | BOI report updates (any ownership/info changes) | FinCEN |
| Within 20 days | Texas New Hire Reporting | TX Attorney General |
