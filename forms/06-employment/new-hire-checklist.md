## Texas State Requirements

### New Hire Reporting
- [ ] **Report to Texas Attorney General within 20 calendar days of hire**
- [ ] Filing methods:
  - Online: https://portal.cs.oag.state.tx.us/wps/portal/employerportal
  - Fax: (800) 732-5015
  - Mail: New Hire Reporting, P.O. Box 149224, Austin, TX 78714-9224
- [ ] Required information: Employee name, SSN, address, hire date; Employer name, address, EIN
- [ ] Date reported: [DATE]

### Texas Income Tax
- **Texas does NOT have a state income tax.** No state withholding form is required.

## Workplace Compliance

### Required Posters (Must Be Displayed)
- [ ] Federal Minimum Wage (FLSA)
- [ ] OSHA "Job Safety and Health" poster
- [ ] Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO)
- [ ] Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA) -- if 50+ employees
- [ ] Employee Polygraph Protection Act (EPPA)
- [ ] USERRA (Uniformed Services)
- [ ] Texas Payday Law notice
- [ ] Texas Workers' Compensation notice (if covered)

### Applicable Laws
- [ ] FLSA: Minimum wage ($7.25 federal / TX follows federal) and overtime (1.5x after 40 hrs/week)
- [ ] Title VII (if 15+ employees): Anti-discrimination
- [ ] Texas Labor Code Chapter 21: Texas anti-discrimination
- [ ] ADA (if 15+ employees): Disability accommodation
- [ ] ADEA (if 20+ employees): Age discrimination
- [ ] Workers' compensation: Not mandatory in Texas, but recommended
