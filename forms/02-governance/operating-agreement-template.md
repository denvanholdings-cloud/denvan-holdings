## ARTICLE II: DEFINITIONS

**2.1** "Agreement" means this Company Agreement, as amended from time to time.

**2.2** "BOC" means the Texas Business Organizations Code, as amended.

**2.3** "Capital Contribution" means the total amount of cash and fair market value of property contributed by a Member.

**2.4** "Distributable Cash" means cash available for distribution after payment of all Company obligations and establishment of reasonable reserves.

**2.5** "Manager" means any person designated as a manager of the Company under this Agreement.

**2.6** "Member" means any person who holds a Membership Interest in the Company.

**2.7** "Membership Interest" means a Member's share of profits, losses, and distributions, together with the Member's right to participate in management as provided herein.

**2.8** "Percentage Interest" means a Member's percentage ownership interest as set forth in Exhibit A.

**2.9** "Transfer" means any sale, assignment, pledge, encumbrance, gift, or other disposition of a Membership Interest.

## ARTICLE V: CAPITAL CONTRIBUTIONS & ACCOUNTS

**5.1 Initial Capital Contributions.** Each Member shall contribute the amount of capital set forth in **Exhibit A**.

**5.2 Additional Contributions.** No Member shall be required to make additional Capital Contributions without the Member's written consent.

**5.3 Capital Accounts.** A separate Capital Account shall be maintained for each Member in accordance with Treasury Regulation Section 1.704-1(b)(2)(iv).

**5.4 No Interest.** No interest shall be paid on Capital Contributions or Capital Account balances.

**5.5 No Withdrawal.** No Member shall be entitled to withdraw any portion of the Member's Capital Contribution without the consent of the Manager(s).

---

## ARTICLE VI: ALLOCATIONS & DISTRIBUTIONS

**6.1 Profits and Losses.** Net profits and net losses of the Company shall be allocated among the Members in proportion to their respective Percentage Interests.

**6.2 Distributions.** Distributions of Distributable Cash shall be made to the Members in proportion to their respective Percentage Interests, at such times and in such amounts as determined by the Manager(s), subject to:
- (a) Payment of all Company debts and obligations;
- (b) Maintenance of reasonable reserves;
- (c) Compliance with applicable law.

**6.3 Tax Distributions.** The Company shall use reasonable efforts to distribute to each Member, prior to the due date for estimated tax payments, an amount sufficient to cover the Member's estimated tax liability attributable to the Company's income.

**6.4 Limitation.** No distribution shall be made if it would violate the BOC or render the Company unable to pay its debts as they become due.

---

## ARTICLE VII: TRANSFER OF MEMBERSHIP INTERESTS

**7.1 Restrictions on Transfer.** No Member may Transfer all or any portion of the Member's Membership Interest without the prior written consent of Members holding a majority of the Percentage Interests.

**7.2 Right of First Refusal.** Before any Transfer to a third party, the transferring Member must first offer the Membership Interest to the remaining Members on the same terms and conditions. The remaining Members shall have [30] days to accept or decline.

**7.3 Permitted Transfers.** The following Transfers shall not require Member consent:
- (a) Transfer to a revocable trust for the benefit of the Member;
- (b) Transfer to the Member's spouse, children, or grandchildren;
- (c) Transfer by operation of law (death, divorce).

**7.4 Conditions of Transfer.** Any permitted transferee must execute a written agreement to be bound by this Agreement.

---

## ARTICLE VIII: DISSOLUTION & WINDING UP

**8.1 Events of Dissolution.** The Company shall be dissolved upon:
- (a) Written consent of Members holding a majority of the Percentage Interests;
- (b) Entry of a judicial decree of dissolution under the BOC;
- (c) Any event that makes it unlawful for the Company to continue its business;
- (d) Any other event specified in the Certificate of Formation.

**8.2 Winding Up.** Upon dissolution, the Manager(s) shall wind up the Company's affairs by:
- (a) Collecting all assets and receivables;
- (b) Paying all debts, liabilities, and obligations;
- (c) Distributing remaining assets to Members in proportion to their Capital Account balances;
- (d) Filing a Certificate of Termination with the Texas Secretary of State.

**8.3 No Deficit Restoration.** No Member shall be required to restore a deficit Capital Account balance upon dissolution.

---

## ARTICLE IX: BOOKS, RECORDS & ACCOUNTING

**9.1 Fiscal Year.** The fiscal year of the Company shall be the calendar year (January 1 -- December 31).

**9.2 Books and Records.** The Company shall maintain at its principal office:
- (a) A current list of Members and their Membership Interests;
- (b) Copies of federal, state, and local tax returns;
- (c) Copies of this Agreement and all amendments;
- (d) Financial statements for the current and three preceding fiscal years;
- (e) Minutes of all Member and Manager meetings;
- (f) The Certificate of Formation and all amendments.

**9.3 Inspection Rights.** Each Member shall have the right to inspect and copy Company books and records during normal business hours upon reasonable notice (BOC Section 101.502).

**9.4 Tax Matters.** The Manager shall serve as the "Tax Matters Partner" (or "Partnership Representative" under the Bipartisan Budget Act) and shall be authorized to make tax elections and represent the Company before the IRS.

**9.5 Tax Returns.** The Company shall prepare and file all required federal and state tax returns and shall furnish each Member with a Schedule K-1 or equivalent within 75 days of the end of each fiscal year.

---

## ARTICLE X: INDEMNIFICATION & LIABILITY

**10.1 Limited Liability.** No Member shall be personally liable for any debt, obligation, or liability of the Company solely by reason of being a Member (BOC Section 101.114).

**10.2 Indemnification.** The Company shall indemnify and hold harmless each Manager and Member from any loss, damage, or claim arising from acts performed in good faith on behalf of the Company, to the fullest extent permitted by the BOC (BOC Section 8.101 -- 8.103).

**10.3 Standard of Care.** Each Manager shall discharge duties in good faith, with the care an ordinarily prudent person in a like position would exercise, and in a manner reasonably believed to be in the best interest of the Company.

---

## ARTICLE XI: AMENDMENTS

**11.1 Amendments.** This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument signed by Members holding a majority of the Percentage Interests, unless a greater percentage is required by this Agreement or the BOC.

---

## ARTICLE XII: MISCELLANEOUS

**12.1 Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement among the Members and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings.

**12.2 Severability.** If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

**12.3 Notices.** All notices shall be in writing and delivered personally, by certified mail (return receipt requested), or by email to the addresses set forth in Exhibit A.

**12.4 Waiver.** No waiver of any provision of this Agreement shall be effective unless in writing and signed by the waiving party.

**12.5 Binding Effect.** This Agreement shall be binding upon and inure to the benefit of the Members and their respective heirs, successors, and permitted assigns.

**12.6 Counterparts.** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original.
