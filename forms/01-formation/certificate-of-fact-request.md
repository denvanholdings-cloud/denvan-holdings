## What Is a Certificate of Fact?

A Certificate of Fact -- Status (commonly called a "Certificate of Good Standing") is an official document issued by the Texas Secretary of State confirming that an entity:

- Is duly formed or registered in Texas
- Is in existence/good standing
- Has not been forfeited or terminated
- Has satisfied all applicable filing requirements with the SOS

**Note:** The Certificate of Fact does NOT verify franchise tax compliance. Tax standing must be confirmed separately with the Texas Comptroller.

1. [ ] Go to https://direct.sos.state.tx.us
2. [ ] Log in or create an account
3. [ ] Navigate to "Certifications" or "Certificate of Fact"
4. [ ] Search for entity: Denvan Holdings LLC
5. [ ] Select "Certificate of Fact -- Status"
6. [ ] Pay fee: **$15.00** (credit card)
7. [ ] Download certificate immediately (PDF)

1. [ ] Write a letter requesting a Certificate of Fact -- Status for:

3. [ ] Mail to:
   - Secretary of State

4. [ ] Processing time: Approximately 5-7 business days
