## 1. Overview

Texas imposes a franchise tax (margin tax) on all taxable entities formed, organized, or doing business in Texas. This is a privilege tax for the right to do business in Texas -- it is NOT an income tax.

**Comptroller Website:** https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/franchise/
**WebFile Portal:** https://webfile.cpa.texas.gov

---

## 2. Who Must File

- **ALL LLCs** formed or registered in Texas must file, regardless of revenue
- Holding companies with no operating revenue must still file
- Even entities with zero revenue must file a No Tax Due Report + Public Information Report
- Failure to file can result in **forfeiture of the right to do business in Texas**

## 7. Penalties

| Violation | Penalty |
|-----------|---------|
| 1-30 days late | 5% of tax due |
| 31+ days late | 10% of tax due |
| Failure to file | Entity may be **forfeited** by Texas SOS |

**Reinstatement** after forfeiture requires: all past-due reports, all taxes/penalties/interest, plus reinstatement fees.
