## 2. Tax Classification Election

An LLC defaults to pass-through taxation. Elections to change classification:

| Classification | Default | Election Form | Deadline |
|---------------|---------|---------------|----------|
| Single-member LLC | Disregarded entity | N/A (default) | -- |
| Multi-member LLC | Partnership | N/A (default) | -- |
| S-Corporation | Must elect | **Form 2553** | Within 75 days of formation or by March 15 |
| C-Corporation | Must elect | **Form 8832** | Within 75 days of desired effective date |
