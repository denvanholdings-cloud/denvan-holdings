## Changes That Require a BOI Update

- Change in beneficial owner (new owner added or existing owner removed)
- Change in ownership percentage (crossing 25% threshold)
- Change in beneficial owner's legal name
- Change in beneficial owner's residential address
- Change in beneficial owner's identifying document (new DL, passport, etc.)
- Change in company legal name
- Change in company address
- Addition of new DBA / trade name
- Removal of DBA / trade name
- Change in registered agent (if affects reported info)
- New company applicant information (if applicable)
- Any correction to previously reported information

## Reminders

- Updates must be filed within **30 days** of the change
- Corrections must be filed within **30 days** of discovering an error
- Willful failure to update: up to $500/day (civil) or $10,000 + 2 years (criminal)
- Review this log quarterly to ensure no changes were missed
- When in doubt, file an update -- there is no fee
