## 1. Overview

The Corporate Transparency Act requires most domestic LLCs and corporations to report beneficial ownership information to FinCEN. The purpose is to combat money laundering, terrorist financing, and other illicit financial activity by creating a national database of beneficial owners.

- **Filing fee:** None (free)
- **Filing method:** Online only at https://boiefiling.fincen.gov

## 5. Beneficial Owner Information

A "beneficial owner" is any individual who:
1. **Directly or indirectly owns or controls 25% or more** of the ownership interests, OR
2. **Exercises substantial control** over the entity (e.g., senior officers, decision-makers, anyone with authority over important decisions)

> Officers such as CEO, CFO, COO, or anyone with similar authority are considered to exercise "substantial control" regardless of ownership percentage.
