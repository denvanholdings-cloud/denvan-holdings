| **LinkedIn** | Primary | Corporate updates, leadership, industry insights | 3-5x/week |

## SEO Strategy

### Primary Keywords

| Keyword | Search Volume | Difficulty | Priority |
|---------|--------------|------------|----------|
| holding company services | Medium | Low | High |
| multi-industry business management | Low | Low | High |
| business holding company | Medium | Medium | High |
| corporate parent company | Medium | Low | High |
| professional services holding company | Low | Low | High |
| subsidiary management | Low | Low | Medium |
| business portfolio management | Medium | Medium | Medium |
| corporate consulting services | Medium | High | Medium |

### Long-Tail Keywords
- "how to structure a holding company with subsidiaries"
- "holding company for small businesses"
- "multi-industry holding company benefits"
- "parent company subsidiary management services"
- "professional services and technology consulting firm"
- "corporate holding company for entrepreneurs"
- "diversified business portfolio management"

### SEO Implementation

1. **On-Page SEO**
   - Unique title tags for every page (under 60 characters)
   - Meta descriptions for every page (under 160 characters)
   - H1-H3 hierarchy using target keywords naturally
   - Internal linking between pages
   - Image alt text on all images
   - Schema markup (Organization, LocalBusiness)

2. **Technical SEO**
   - Fast page load (<3 seconds)
   - Mobile-responsive design
   - SSL certificate (HTTPS)
   - XML sitemap submitted to Google Search Console
   - Clean URL structure
   - Structured data markup

3. **Content SEO**
   - Monthly blog posts targeting long-tail keywords
   - Service pages optimized for primary keywords
   - FAQ sections with schema markup
   - Case studies and success stories

4. **Local SEO**
   - Google Business Profile for Denvan Holdings
   - Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across directories
   - Local business citations (Yelp, BBB, industry directories)
