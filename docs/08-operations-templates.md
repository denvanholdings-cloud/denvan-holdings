### Outbound Call Script (Follow-Up on Inquiry)

```

"Thank you for your time, [Name]. Looking forward to connecting."
```

## Standard Operating Procedures (SOP Templates)

### SOP: Handling Inbound Inquiries

```
TRIGGER: New contact form submission or phone call

STEP 1: Log the inquiry
- Add contact to HubSpot CRM
- Note: name, email, phone, inquiry type, message

STEP 2: Auto-reply
- Send welcome/auto-reply email template (see above)
- Confirm within 1-2 business days response time

STEP 3: Review and assign
- Determine inquiry type (general, partnership, investor, career)
- Assign to appropriate team member
- Set follow-up reminder in CRM

STEP 4: Respond
- Call or email within 24 hours (business days)
- Use appropriate call script or email template
- Log the interaction in CRM

STEP 5: Follow up
- Send follow-up email after call (template above)
- Schedule next touchpoint
- Update CRM deal stage if applicable

TIMELINE: First response within 24 business hours
OWNER: [Assigned team member]
```

### SOP: Onboarding a New Client (Subsidiary Level)

```
TRIGGER: Client signs agreement / makes payment

STEP 1: Welcome
- Send client onboarding email (template above)
- Add to CRM as active client
- Create client folder in Google Drive

STEP 2: Intake
- Schedule kickoff call
- Send intake questionnaire (Google Form)
- Collect all required information and documents

STEP 3: Setup
- Set up client in project management tool
- Create communication channel (email thread or chat)
- Assign team members to client

STEP 4: Kickoff
- Conduct kickoff meeting
- Review scope, timeline, deliverables
- Set expectations for communication cadence

STEP 5: Deliver
- Begin service delivery per agreement
- Send progress updates per agreed schedule
- Track time and expenses

STEP 6: Invoice
- Send invoice per billing schedule
- Follow up on unpaid invoices at 7, 14, 30 days
- Log payment in QuickBooks

TIMELINE: Onboarding complete within 5 business days of signing
OWNER: Subsidiary lead
```

### SOP: Launching a New Subsidiary (DBA)

```
TRIGGER: Decision to launch new business line

WEEK 1: Legal & Financial
- [ ] File DBA with county/state
- [ ] Open subsidiary bank account (or sub-account)
- [ ] Set up in QuickBooks as new entity/class
- [ ] Define management fee structure

WEEK 2: Branding & Digital
- [ ] Create subsidiary name and tagline
- [ ] Design logo using brand framework
- [ ] Set up email addresses
- [ ] Create website section or landing page
- [ ] Set up social media profiles

WEEK 3: Operations
- [ ] Define service offerings and pricing
- [ ] Create service agreement template
- [ ] Write key SOPs (intake, delivery, invoicing)
- [ ] Set up scheduling page (Calendly)
- [ ] Create sales materials (one-pager, pitch)

WEEK 4: Launch
- [ ] Soft launch to warm network
- [ ] Collect feedback and adjust
- [ ] Full public launch
- [ ] Announce on parent company channels
- [ ] Begin marketing campaigns

OWNER: CEO / COO
REVIEW: Monthly performance review for first 6 months
```

## Meeting Templates

### Weekly Team Check-In (30 min)

```
AGENDA:
1. Wins from this week (5 min)
   - Each person shares one win

2. Subsidiary updates (10 min)
   - Revenue / pipeline status
   - Client updates
   - Any blockers

3. Parent company updates (5 min)
   - Corporate initiatives
   - Shared services updates

4. Priorities for next week (5 min)
   - Top 3 priorities per person

5. Open items (5 min)
   - Questions, ideas, support needed
```

### Monthly Business Review (60 min)

```
AGENDA:
1. Financial review (15 min)
   - Revenue vs. target (per subsidiary + consolidated)
   - Expenses and profit margins
   - Cash flow status
   - Outstanding invoices

2. Subsidiary performance (20 min)
   - KPIs per subsidiary
   - Client acquisition / retention
   - Wins and challenges
   - Pipeline and forecasts

3. Operations review (10 min)
   - Systems and tools -- any issues?
   - Process improvements needed
   - Team capacity

4. Strategic discussion (10 min)
   - New opportunities
   - Market trends
   - Portfolio adjustments

5. Action items (5 min)
   - Assign owners and deadlines
```

### Quarterly Strategic Planning (90 min)

```
AGENDA:
1. Quarter in review (20 min)
   - Revenue and growth summary
   - Goal completion scorecard
   - Key lessons learned

2. Portfolio assessment (20 min)
   - Each subsidiary: keep, grow, pivot, or sunset
   - New subsidiary opportunities
   - Acquisition targets

3. Financial planning (15 min)
   - Next quarter budget
   - Capital allocation decisions
   - Investment priorities

4. Strategy and goals (20 min)
   - Top 3 corporate goals for next quarter
   - Per-subsidiary goals
   - Marketing and growth initiatives

5. Action plan (15 min)
   - Specific actions, owners, deadlines
   - Resource needs
   - Risk mitigation
```
