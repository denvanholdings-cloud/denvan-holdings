## 3. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS

**3.1** Contractor is an independent contractor, not an employee of the Company. Contractor is responsible for:
- Self-employment taxes (FICA, Medicare)
- Personal income taxes
- Own insurance and benefits
- Own tools, equipment, and workspace (unless otherwise agreed)

**3.2** The Company will not:
- Withhold taxes from payments
- Provide employee benefits
- Provide workers' compensation
- Control how, when, or where Contractor performs the work (only the results)

**3.3** Contractor may work for other clients simultaneously, provided it does not create a conflict of interest or interfere with the services under this Agreement.

---

## 4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

**4.1 Work Made for Hire.** All work product, deliverables, and materials created by Contractor in connection with this Agreement shall be considered "work made for hire" and shall be the sole and exclusive property of the Company.

**4.2 Assignment.** To the extent any work product does not qualify as work made for hire, Contractor hereby assigns all rights, title, and interest in such work to the Company.

**4.3 Pre-Existing IP.** Contractor retains ownership of any pre-existing intellectual property. If Contractor incorporates pre-existing IP into deliverables, Contractor grants the Company a perpetual, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use it.

---

## 5. CONFIDENTIALITY

**5.1** Contractor agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all Company information, including business strategies, financial data, customer lists, processes, and any information not publicly available.

**5.2** This obligation survives termination of this Agreement for a period of **[2] years**.

**5.3** Contractor shall not use Company confidential information for any purpose other than performing services under this Agreement.

---

## 6. NON-SOLICITATION

During the term of this Agreement and for **[12] months** after termination, Contractor shall not directly solicit or attempt to hire any employee or contractor of the Company, or solicit any client of the Company for a competing service.

---

## 7. TERMINATION

**7.1** Either party may terminate this Agreement with **[14] days** written notice.

**7.2** The Company may terminate immediately if Contractor breaches any material term of this Agreement.

**7.3** Upon termination:
- Contractor shall deliver all completed and in-progress work
- Company shall pay for all services rendered through the termination date
- All confidentiality obligations remain in effect

---

## 8. INDEMNIFICATION

## 9. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
