## Slide 7: Competitive Advantage

### What Makes Us Different

1. **DBA Model Efficiency**
   - Launch new businesses in days, not months
   - Lower legal costs than separate LLC for each business
   - Centralized operations under one entity

2. **Shared Services at Scale**
   - Each new subsidiary reduces per-unit overhead
   - Marginal cost of adding a subsidiary decreases over time
   - Cross-subsidiary synergies create value

3. **Disciplined Evaluation Process**
   - Rigorous scoring framework for new opportunities
   - Data-driven performance management
   - Quick identification of underperformers

4. **Diverse Revenue Base**
   - Not dependent on any single industry
   - Management fees provide baseline predictable income
   - Multiple growth levers available
