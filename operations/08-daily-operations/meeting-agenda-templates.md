# Meeting Agenda Templates

---

## 1. Weekly Subsidiary Check-In (15 minutes)

**Frequency:** Weekly (Monday)
**Attendees:** Subsidiary Lead + Parent Company
**Format:** Phone or video call

| Time | Topic |
|------|-------|
| 0-3 min | Revenue update: How did last week go? |
| 3-6 min | Top wins: What went well? |
| 6-9 min | Blockers: What's holding you back? |
| 9-12 min | This week: Top 3 priorities |
| 12-15 min | Support needed from parent company |

**Follow-Up:** Subsidiary lead sends 3-bullet email summary after call.

---

## 2. Monthly Performance Review (45 minutes)

**Frequency:** Monthly (last week of month)
**Attendees:** Subsidiary Lead + CEO + Finance
**Preparation:** Financial report delivered by 10th; review before meeting

| Time | Topic |
|------|-------|
| 0-5 min | Overview: How was the month overall? |
| 5-15 min | Financial review: Revenue, expenses, profit, vs. budget |
| 15-25 min | KPI review: Customers, pipeline, marketing, operations |
| 25-35 min | Challenges: What's not working? Root causes? |
| 35-40 min | Next month goals: 3-5 specific, measurable targets |
| 40-45 min | Action items: Who does what by when? |

**Follow-Up:** CEO sends written summary with action items within 24 hours.

---

## 3. Quarterly Strategic Planning (2 hours)

**Frequency:** Quarterly (first week of quarter)
**Attendees:** All Subsidiary Leads + CEO + Finance
**Preparation:** Quarterly financial report, KPI dashboard, updated market research

| Time | Topic |
|------|-------|
| 0-10 min | Opening: Quarter in review (CEO overview) |
| 10-30 min | Financial review: Portfolio-wide P&L, budget vs. actual |
| 30-50 min | Subsidiary reports: Each lead gives 5-min update |
| 50-60 min | Break |
| 60-80 min | Cross-subsidiary opportunities: Referrals, collaboration |
| 80-100 min | Strategic discussion: Market trends, competitive landscape |
| 100-115 min | Next quarter goals: Portfolio-wide + per subsidiary |
| 115-120 min | Action items and closing |

**Follow-Up:** CEO distributes quarterly plan document within 3 days.

---

## 4. Annual Portfolio Review (Half Day - 4 hours)

**Frequency:** Annually (December or January)
**Attendees:** All Subsidiary Leads + CEO + Finance + Advisory Board (if applicable)
**Preparation:** Annual financial statements, growth strategy doc, market research

| Time | Topic |
|------|-------|
| 0-15 min | Opening: Year in review (CEO presentation) |
| 15-45 min | Financial review: Full-year P&L, balance sheet, cash flow |
| 45-75 min | Subsidiary deep dives: Each lead presents (10 min each) |
| 75-90 min | Break |
| 90-120 min | Portfolio evaluation: Which subs are performing? Which need attention? |
| 120-150 min | Growth strategy: New opportunities, acquisitions, expansion |
| 150-180 min | Budget planning: Next year's budget discussion |
| 180-210 min | Goals and milestones: Next year targets |
| 210-240 min | Action items, assignments, and closing |

**Follow-Up:** CEO distributes annual plan and budget within 1 week.

---

## 5. New Business Evaluation Meeting (60 minutes)

**Frequency:** As needed
**Attendees:** CEO + Finance + relevant team members
**Preparation:** Completed Subsidiary Evaluation Scorecard, market research

| Time | Topic |
|------|-------|
| 0-10 min | Opportunity overview: What's the business concept? |
| 10-20 min | Market analysis: Size, competition, demand |
| 20-30 min | Financial projections: Startup costs, revenue timeline, margins |
| 30-40 min | Operational fit: Can we support this with current infrastructure? |
| 40-50 min | Risk assessment: What could go wrong? Mitigation strategies? |
| 50-55 min | Scorecard review: Walk through the evaluation scores |
| 55-60 min | Decision: GO / CONDITIONAL GO / HOLD / NO GO |

**Follow-Up:** Document decision and next steps. If GO, initiate launch checklist.

---

## Meeting Rules

1. **Start and end on time.** Respect everyone's schedule.
2. **Come prepared.** Review materials before the meeting.
3. **One speaker at a time.** Listen actively.
4. **Document action items.** Every meeting ends with: Who does What by When.
5. **Follow up within 24 hours.** Send summary and action items to all attendees.
6. **Cancel if not needed.** If there's nothing to discuss, give time back.
