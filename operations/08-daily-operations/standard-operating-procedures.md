# Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

---

## SOP 1: New Client Onboarding

**Purpose:** Ensure every new client receives a consistent, professional onboarding experience.

### Steps:
1. Client signs contract or agreement
2. Send welcome email within 24 hours:
   - Thank them for choosing us
   - Outline next steps and timeline
   - Provide key contact information
   - Attach any onboarding forms needed
3. Create client record in CRM
4. Set up project in project management tool
5. Schedule kickoff call (within 3 business days)
6. Conduct kickoff call:
   - Introduce team members
   - Review scope and expectations
   - Confirm timeline and milestones
   - Answer questions
7. Send kickoff summary email with action items
8. Begin service delivery

**Owner:** Subsidiary Lead
**Timeline:** Complete within 5 business days of signing

## SOP 2: Invoice Processing

**Purpose:** Ensure all invoices are processed accurately and on time.

### Incoming Invoices (Paying Vendors):
1. Receive invoice via email or mail
2. Verify: correct vendor, amount, and description
3. Match to purchase order or approved expense
4. Code to correct account and subsidiary class in QuickBooks
5. Route for approval:
   - Under $1,000: Subsidiary Lead approves
   - $1,000-$10,000: Parent Company approves
   - Over $10,000: Managing Member approves
6. Schedule payment per terms (Net 15, Net 30)
7. Record payment in QuickBooks
8. File invoice in Google Drive: `Financial/Invoices/[Year]/[Month]/`

### Outgoing Invoices (Billing Clients):
1. Generate invoice in QuickBooks
2. Review for accuracy (client info, amounts, descriptions)
3. Send via QuickBooks email or PDF
4. Record in accounts receivable
5. Follow up on unpaid invoices:
   - Day 7: Friendly reminder email
   - Day 14: Second reminder with phone call
   - Day 30: Final notice -- escalate to Managing Member

**Owner:** Finance Team
**Timeline:** Process incoming within 48 hours; send outgoing within 2 days of service

## SOP 3: Expense Approval

**Purpose:** Ensure all expenses are authorized and properly documented.

### Process:
1. Person requesting expense submits: description, amount, purpose, receipt
2. Approval routing:
   - Under $500: Direct manager approves
   - $500-$5,000: Parent Company approves
   - Over $5,000: Managing Member approves
3. Approved expenses are coded and recorded in QuickBooks
4. Receipt stored in Google Drive: `Financial/Receipts/[Year]/[Month]/`
5. Reimbursements (if personal card was used) processed with next payroll

**Rule:** No expense over $100 without receipt. No expense over $500 without pre-approval.

## SOP 4: Social Media Posting

**Purpose:** Maintain consistent, professional social media presence.

### Process:
1. Content created per weekly content calendar
2. Draft post with copy + image/graphic
3. Review for brand voice compliance and accuracy
4. Schedule using Buffer/Hootsuite
5. Monitor for engagement within 2 hours of posting
6. Respond to all comments and messages within 24 hours
7. Track metrics weekly (engagement rate, reach, followers)

### Content Approval:
- Standard posts (educational, quotes, updates): No approval needed
- Company announcements: Managing Member approval required
- Paid promotions: Marketing lead + Managing Member approval

**Owner:** Marketing
**Frequency:** Schedule all posts for the week by Friday EOD

## SOP 5: Customer Complaint Resolution

**Purpose:** Resolve customer issues quickly and professionally.

### Steps:
1. **Acknowledge** the complaint within 4 hours (email or phone)
2. **Listen** and document the issue completely
3. **Apologize** sincerely for the inconvenience
4. **Investigate** the root cause (within 24 hours)
5. **Resolve:** Propose a solution and get customer agreement
6. **Execute** the resolution within the committed timeframe
7. **Follow up** within 48 hours to confirm satisfaction
8. **Document** in CRM: issue, resolution, and any systemic changes needed
9. **Review** if complaint reveals a process improvement opportunity

### Escalation:
- Level 1: Subsidiary Lead handles directly
- Level 2: Parent Company involved (if unresolved after 48 hours)
- Level 3: Managing Member involved (legal risk, high-value client, reputation risk)

**Target:** Resolve 90% of complaints within 48 hours.

## SOP 7: New Employee Tech Setup

**Purpose:** Get new employees productive on day one.

### Checklist (complete before start date):
1. [ ] Create Google Workspace account ([name]@denvanholdings.com)
2. [ ] Add to relevant Google Drive shared folders
3. [ ] Add to Slack/Chat channels
4. [ ] Add to project management tool (Asana/Monday/Notion)
5. [ ] Create accounts for role-specific tools
6. [ ] Set up 2FA on all accounts
7. [ ] Add to password manager shared vault
8. [ ] Send welcome email with all login credentials
9. [ ] Schedule IT orientation (30 min) for first day
10. [ ] Order any physical equipment if needed

### Offboarding (same day as separation):
1. [ ] Revoke Google Workspace access
2. [ ] Remove from all shared folders and channels
3. [ ] Change passwords on shared accounts
4. [ ] Remove from project management and CRM
5. [ ] Collect any company devices
6. [ ] Forward email to designated person for 30 days

**Owner:** IT / Admin
**Timeline:** Setup within 24 hours of hire confirmation; offboarding same day as separation
