# Operational Checklists

## Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, and Annual Tasks

---

## DAILY Checklist

- [ ] Check and respond to emails (info@, accounting@, hr@)
- [ ] Check voicemail and return calls
- [ ] Review subsidiary dashboards / revenue updates
- [ ] Process any incoming invoices or payments
- [ ] Respond to customer/client inquiries (within 24 hours)
- [ ] Check social media for messages and engagement
- [ ] Review task list and prioritize the day
- [ ] Log any expenses with receipts

---

## WEEKLY Checklist (Every Monday)

### Operations
- [ ] Subsidiary check-in calls (15 min each)
- [ ] Review weekly revenue across all subsidiaries
- [ ] Update customer pipeline / CRM
- [ ] Follow up on outstanding proposals or leads
- [ ] Review and approve any pending expenses

### Marketing
- [ ] Schedule social media posts for the week
- [ ] Publish or finalize blog content
- [ ] Review social media analytics from last week
- [ ] Respond to comments and messages

### Finance
- [ ] Quick review of bank account balances
- [ ] Process accounts payable (pay outstanding bills)
- [ ] Send any outstanding invoices

### Administration
- [ ] Review upcoming deadlines and calendar
- [ ] Update project management board
- [ ] File any documents or receipts
- [ ] Team communication / announcements

---

## MONTHLY Checklist (First Two Weeks of Month)

### Financial Close (by the 10th)
- [ ] Reconcile all bank accounts
- [ ] Reconcile credit cards
- [ ] Calculate and invoice management fees to subsidiaries
- [ ] Categorize all transactions in QuickBooks
- [ ] Generate P&L per subsidiary
- [ ] Generate consolidated P&L
- [ ] Generate balance sheet
- [ ] Update cash flow forecast
- [ ] Transfer tax reserve (25-30% of net profit)
- [ ] Review actual vs. budget variances
- [ ] File monthly financial report

### Subsidiary Reviews
- [ ] Conduct monthly performance review for each subsidiary
- [ ] Review KPI dashboards
- [ ] Identify any underperforming areas
- [ ] Set goals for next month
- [ ] Document action items from reviews

### Marketing
- [ ] Review monthly marketing analytics
- [ ] Analyze website traffic trends
- [ ] Review email marketing performance
- [ ] Plan next month's content calendar
- [ ] Evaluate paid advertising ROI (if running ads)

### HR & Admin
- [ ] Verify payroll processed correctly
- [ ] Review employee hours and overtime
- [ ] Check for any open positions to fill
- [ ] Update document management (file contracts, receipts)

---

## QUARTERLY Checklist

### Strategic Planning
- [ ] Conduct quarterly strategic review (2-hour session)
- [ ] Review portfolio performance across all subsidiaries
- [ ] Evaluate cross-subsidiary synergies and opportunities
- [ ] Assess market conditions and adjust strategy
- [ ] Set goals and KPIs for next quarter
- [ ] Review and update annual forecast

### Financial
- [ ] Submit quarterly estimated tax payment (EFTPS)
- [ ] Full budget vs. actual analysis
- [ ] Cash flow review and 3-month projection
- [ ] Review pricing strategy for each subsidiary
- [ ] Review vendor contracts for renewal/renegotiation

### Growth
- [ ] Evaluate pipeline of new subsidiary opportunities
- [ ] Review acquisition opportunities (if any)
- [ ] Assess capacity for new launches
- [ ] Update growth strategy document

### Compliance
- [ ] Review insurance coverage (adequate for current operations?)
- [ ] Check business license and permit status
- [ ] Review contractor agreements for compliance
- [ ] Cybersecurity review (passwords, access, backups)

---

## ANNUAL Checklist

### Tax & Financial (January - April)
- [ ] Issue 1099 forms to contractors (by January 31)
- [ ] Compile annual financial statements
- [ ] Meet with CPA for tax preparation
- [ ] File federal tax return (by April 15 or extension)
- [ ] File Texas Franchise Tax report (by May 15)
- [ ] Review and update chart of accounts if needed

### Strategic (November - December for Next Year)
- [ ] Annual strategic planning session (half day)
- [ ] Set annual revenue and growth targets
- [ ] Create next year's budget
- [ ] Evaluate S-Corp election (if not yet elected)
- [ ] Review and update business plan

### Operations
- [ ] Review and update Employee Handbook
- [ ] Review and update all SOPs
- [ ] Conduct annual performance reviews for all employees
- [ ] Review compensation and adjust as needed
- [ ] Update emergency contact and key person documentation

### Compliance & Insurance
- [ ] Renew business insurance policies
- [ ] Renew registered agent (if using a service)
- [ ] Check DBA expiration dates
- [ ] Review all active contracts and agreements
- [ ] Update business continuity plan

### Marketing
- [ ] Annual marketing strategy review
- [ ] Update website content and design
- [ ] Review brand guidelines
- [ ] Analyze full-year marketing ROI
- [ ] Set next year's marketing budget and plan
