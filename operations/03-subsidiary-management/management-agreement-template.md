## 1. PURPOSE

The Parent Company agrees to provide management and shared services to the Subsidiary, and the Subsidiary agrees to pay a management fee for these services, as outlined in this Agreement.

---

The Parent Company shall provide the following services to the Subsidiary:

### 2.1 Financial Services
- Monthly bookkeeping and financial reporting
- Accounts receivable and payable management
- Bank account management and reconciliation
- Budget preparation and cash flow forecasting
- Tax preparation and filing support
- Management fee invoicing and collection

### 2.2 Legal & Compliance
- DBA registration and renewal
- Standard contract review and drafting
- Business license and permit management
- Regulatory compliance monitoring
- NDA and agreement templates

### 2.3 Human Resources
- Job posting and recruitment support
- Payroll processing
- Employee onboarding
- Policy and handbook enforcement
- Performance review facilitation

### 2.4 Information Technology
- Business email and communication tools
- Website hosting and maintenance
- Software and tool management
- Cybersecurity and data backup
- Technical support

### 2.5 Marketing & Brand
- Brand strategy and positioning
- Social media management
- Content creation and publishing
- Email marketing campaigns
- SEO and digital marketing
- Marketing analytics and reporting

### 2.6 Strategic Advisory
- Business strategy and planning
- Performance monitoring and KPI tracking
- Market research and competitive analysis
- Growth planning and milestone setting
- Weekly check-ins and monthly reviews

## 3. MANAGEMENT FEE

### 3.1 Fee Structure
The Subsidiary shall pay the Parent Company a management fee of **[PERCENTAGE]% of Gross Monthly Revenue**.

### 3.2 Definition of Gross Revenue
Gross Revenue means all revenue received by the Subsidiary from its business operations, before deduction of any expenses, taxes, or other costs.

### 3.3 Payment Schedule
- Management fees are calculated monthly based on the prior month's gross revenue
- Payment is due by the **15th of the following month**
- Payment shall be made via bank transfer from the Subsidiary sub-account to the Parent Company operating account

### 3.4 Minimum Fee
The minimum monthly management fee shall be $[MINIMUM AMOUNT], regardless of revenue.

### 3.5 Fee Adjustments
The management fee percentage may be adjusted annually upon mutual written agreement. The Parent Company will provide 30 days written notice of any proposed change.

## 5. PERFORMANCE STANDARDS

### 5.1 Parent Company Obligations
- Deliver all shared services within agreed response times
- Provide monthly financial reports by the 10th of each month
- Conduct weekly check-ins and monthly performance reviews
- Maintain confidentiality of Subsidiary business information
- Act in the best interest of both the Subsidiary and the portfolio

### 5.2 Subsidiary Obligations
- Pay management fees on time and in full
- Provide accurate and timely financial and operational data
- Follow Parent Company brand guidelines and policies
- Participate in scheduled reviews and planning sessions
- Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

---

## 6. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 6.1 Term
This Agreement shall commence on [START DATE] and continue for an initial term of [12] months, automatically renewing for successive [12]-month periods unless terminated.

### 6.2 Termination
Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing [90] days written notice. Upon termination:
- All outstanding management fees become immediately due
- Parent Company will provide transition support for [30] days
- Subsidiary must transition off all shared services within [60] days
- All Company property, accounts, and assets remain with the Parent Company

### 6.3 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate immediately upon written notice if the other party:
- Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within [30] days
- Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy
- Engages in illegal activity
