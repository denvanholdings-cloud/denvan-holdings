# New Subsidiary Launch Checklist

## Step-by-Step: Launching a DBA Under Denvan Holdings

---

## Pre-Launch: Evaluation (Week 1)

- [ ] Complete Subsidiary Evaluation Scorecard (see `subsidiary-evaluation-scorecard.md`)
- [ ] Research target market: size, competition, demand, barriers to entry
- [ ] Define the service/product offering
- [ ] Identify target customers and how to reach them
- [ ] Estimate startup costs and time to first revenue
- [ ] Confirm alignment with Denvan Holdings portfolio strategy
- [ ] **GO / NO-GO DECISION** by Managing Member

---

## Phase 1: Registration & Setup (Week 2)

### Legal
- [ ] Register DBA with Travis County Clerk (see `05-legal-templates/dba-registration-guide.md`)
  - Cost: $25 filing fee
  - Processing: 1-3 business days
- [ ] Obtain any industry-specific licenses or permits
- [ ] Draft Management Services Agreement (see `management-agreement-template.md`)
- [ ] Sign Management Services Agreement

### Financial
- [ ] Open subsidiary sub-account at bank (named "DH - [Business Name]")
- [ ] Set up subsidiary class/category in QuickBooks
- [ ] Create initial budget (see `04-financial-operations/budget-template.md`)
- [ ] Set up payment processing (Stripe or Square)
- [ ] Set management fee rate: [___]% of gross revenue

### Technology
- [ ] Create business email: [name]@denvanholdings.com
- [ ] Set up phone number or extension (if separate from main line)
- [ ] Create Google Drive folder: Subsidiaries / [Business Name]
- [ ] Set up any industry-specific software

---

## Phase 2: Brand & Marketing (Week 2-3)

### Branding
- [ ] Define business name, tagline, and positioning
- [ ] Design logo (or variation of parent brand)
- [ ] Choose brand colors and fonts
- [ ] Create business cards (digital or printed)
- [ ] Write one-paragraph business description

### Online Presence
- [ ] Create landing page or section on Denvan Holdings website
- [ ] Set up Google Business Profile
- [ ] Create social media accounts (LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook -- as relevant)
- [ ] Write and publish announcement blog post
- [ ] Set up email signature template

### Marketing Materials
- [ ] Create service/product one-pager (PDF)
- [ ] Draft initial marketing email template
- [ ] Prepare elevator pitch (30-second and 2-minute versions)
- [ ] Identify first 20 potential customers/leads

---

## Phase 3: Operations Setup (Week 3)

- [ ] Write Standard Operating Procedures for core services
- [ ] Set up client onboarding process
- [ ] Create invoicing templates in QuickBooks
- [ ] Set up project management system (Asana, Monday, or Notion)
- [ ] Define service delivery workflow: inquiry → proposal → contract → delivery → invoice → follow-up
- [ ] Set pricing structure and create rate sheet
- [ ] Establish vendor relationships (if needed)
- [ ] Create customer intake form

---

## Phase 4: Soft Launch (Week 3-4)

- [ ] Reach out to first 10 prospects (email + phone)
- [ ] Offer introductory pricing or pilot program (if applicable)
- [ ] Deliver first service/product to 1-3 beta customers
- [ ] Collect feedback and testimonials
- [ ] Refine offering based on feedback
- [ ] Document lessons learned

---

## Phase 5: Full Launch (Month 2)

- [ ] Announce launch on all social media channels
- [ ] Send launch email to full contact list
- [ ] Activate paid marketing (if budget allows)
- [ ] Begin regular content publishing schedule
- [ ] Start tracking KPIs (see below)
- [ ] Schedule first monthly performance review

---

## First 90 Days Milestones

### Day 30
- [ ] DBA registered and all accounts set up
- [ ] Online presence live (website, Google Business, social)
- [ ] First 5+ customer conversations completed
- [ ] At least 1 paying customer or signed contract
- [ ] First management fee paid to parent company

### Day 60
- [ ] 3-5 active customers
- [ ] Monthly revenue: $[TARGET]
- [ ] All SOPs documented
- [ ] Marketing running consistently (weekly content)
- [ ] First monthly performance review completed

### Day 90
- [ ] 5-10 active customers
- [ ] Monthly revenue on track toward $[TARGET]
- [ ] Positive customer feedback / testimonials
- [ ] Referral system in place
- [ ] Break-even analysis completed
- [ ] Quarterly strategic review with parent company

---

## KPIs to Track from Day 1

| Metric | Target (Month 3) | How to Track |
|--------|------------------|-------------|
| Monthly Revenue | $[TARGET] | QuickBooks |
| Number of Customers | 5-10 | CRM / Spreadsheet |
| Customer Acquisition Cost | < $[TARGET] | Marketing spend / new customers |
| Profit Margin | 25%+ | QuickBooks P&L |
| Management Fee Payment | 100% on time | Bank records |
| Customer Satisfaction | 4.5+ / 5 | Survey / reviews |
| Website Traffic | Growing weekly | Google Analytics |
| Social Media Followers | Growing weekly | Platform analytics |
