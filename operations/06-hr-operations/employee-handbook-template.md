# Employee Handbook

## Denvan Holdings LLC

---

## Welcome

Welcome to Denvan Holdings LLC. We're a multi-industry holding company based in Austin, Texas, dedicated to building, managing, and growing businesses across diverse sectors. This handbook outlines our policies, expectations, and the resources available to you as a team member.

---

## 1. Employment Basics

### At-Will Employment
Employment with Denvan Holdings LLC is "at-will," meaning either the employee or the Company may end the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause or notice. Nothing in this handbook creates a contract of employment.

### Equal Opportunity
Denvan Holdings is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, veteran status, sexual orientation, gender identity, or any other protected characteristic under federal, state, or local law.

### Immigration Law Compliance
All employees must complete Form I-9 and provide proof of eligibility to work in the United States within 3 business days of their start date.

---

## 2. Anti-Harassment & Anti-Discrimination

Denvan Holdings maintains a zero-tolerance policy for harassment, discrimination, or retaliation of any kind. This includes sexual harassment, verbal or physical harassment, and any behavior that creates a hostile work environment.

**Reporting:** If you experience or witness harassment, report it immediately to your manager or the Managing Member. All reports will be investigated promptly and confidentially. Retaliation against anyone who reports in good faith is strictly prohibited.

## 3. Work Hours & Attendance

### Standard Hours
- Standard work week: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM CST
- Core hours (when all team members should be available): 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM CST
- Flexibility may be available depending on role and manager approval

### Remote Work
Remote work arrangements may be available with manager approval. Remote employees are expected to maintain the same productivity, communication, and availability standards as in-office employees.

### Attendance
Regular attendance is essential. If you will be absent or late, notify your manager as early as possible. Excessive unexcused absences may result in disciplinary action.

## 7. Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to:
- Act with honesty, integrity, and professionalism
- Treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect
- Protect Company and client confidential information
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
- Use Company resources responsibly
- Represent Denvan Holdings positively in all interactions

### Conflicts of Interest
Employees must disclose any outside business interests, relationships, or activities that may conflict with their duties at Denvan Holdings. When in doubt, disclose.

---

## 8. Technology & Social Media

### Company Technology
- Company devices and accounts are for business use
- Do not install unauthorized software
- Use strong, unique passwords and enable 2FA
- Report any security incidents immediately
- Company reserves the right to monitor company-owned devices and accounts

### Personal Social Media
- Do not share confidential Company information on personal social media
- If you identify yourself as a Denvan Holdings employee online, make clear that views are your own
- Do not disparage the Company, colleagues, or clients publicly

---

## 9. Confidentiality

All employees have access to confidential business information. You agree to:
- Not disclose confidential information to anyone outside the Company
- Not use confidential information for personal gain
- Return all Company materials upon separation
- This obligation continues after employment ends

Violation of confidentiality may result in immediate termination and legal action.

---

## 10. Separation of Employment

### Voluntary Resignation
Please provide at least [2 weeks] written notice. The Company may accept immediate resignation at its discretion.

### Involuntary Termination
The Company may terminate employment at any time for any lawful reason. Grounds for immediate termination include: theft, fraud, harassment, violence, substance abuse, gross insubordination, or breach of confidentiality.

### Final Pay
Texas law requires final paychecks within 6 days of termination (involuntary) or the next regular pay date (voluntary resignation).

### Exit Process
- Return all Company property (devices, keys, badges, documents)
- Complete knowledge transfer
- Revoke access to all Company systems and accounts
- Exit interview (optional but encouraged)
