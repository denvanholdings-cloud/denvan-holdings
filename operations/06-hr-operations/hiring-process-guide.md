## Step 3: Screen Applicants

### Initial Screening Criteria
- [ ] Resume received and readable
- [ ] Meets minimum qualifications
- [ ] Located in acceptable geography
- [ ] Salary expectations aligned with budget
- [ ] No red flags (unexplained gaps, inconsistencies)

### Phone Screen (15-20 minutes)
- Tell me about yourself and your current situation
- What interests you about this role / Denvan Holdings?
- What are your salary expectations?
- What is your availability to start?
- Do you have any questions for me?

**Pass/Fail:** Move forward or decline within 48 hours.

---

## Step 4: Interview

### Interview Format
- **Round 1:** Video or in-person interview with hiring manager (45-60 min)
- **Round 2:** Meeting with Managing Member / team (30-45 min)
- **Optional Round 3:** Skills assessment or work sample

### General Interview Questions
1. Walk me through your experience with [relevant skill].
2. Describe a time you solved a difficult problem at work.
3. How do you prioritize when you have multiple deadlines?
4. What does excellent work look like to you?
5. Tell me about a time you had to learn something quickly.
6. How do you handle constructive feedback?
7. Where do you see yourself in 2-3 years?
8. What questions do you have about Denvan Holdings?

- [Question about relevant experience]
- [Scenario-based question related to daily work]

## Step 7: Onboard New Employee

### First Day Checklist
- [ ] Workstation / equipment ready
- [ ] Email account created ([name]@denvanholdings.com)
- [ ] Added to Slack/Chat, calendar, shared drives
- [ ] Welcome email sent with first-day logistics
- [ ] Employee handbook provided (get signed acknowledgment)
- [ ] I-9 and W-4 forms completed
- [ ] Direct deposit set up
- [ ] Benefits enrollment initiated (if applicable)
- [ ] Assigned to supervisor/mentor
- [ ] First week schedule shared

### First Week
- [ ] Company overview and culture orientation
- [ ] Team introductions
- [ ] Role expectations and 30/60/90 day goals discussed
- [ ] Shared services orientation
- [ ] Tools and systems training
- [ ] First project or assignment given

### First 30 Days
- [ ] Weekly 1:1 check-ins with manager
- [ ] 30-day check-in: how's it going? Any concerns?
- [ ] Performance expectations clarified
- [ ] Feedback given and received

### First 90 Days
- [ ] Formal 90-day performance review
- [ ] Goals for next quarter set
- [ ] Confirm long-term fit (both sides)
