## QuickBooks Invoice Setup

To create these in QuickBooks Online:
1. Go to **Sales > Invoices > Create Invoice**
2. Use **Custom Form Styles** to match Denvan Holdings branding
3. Set up **Recurring Invoices** for monthly management fees
4. Configure payment terms: Net 15 (internal) or Net 30 (external)
5. Enable online payments (ACH + credit card) for external clients
