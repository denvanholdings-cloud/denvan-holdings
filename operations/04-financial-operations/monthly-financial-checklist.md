# Monthly Financial Checklist

## Month-End Close Process (Complete by 10th of Following Month)

---

### Week 1 of New Month (Days 1-5): Data Collection

- [ ] Download bank statements for all accounts
- [ ] Download credit card statements
- [ ] Collect all outstanding invoices and receipts
- [ ] Confirm all subsidiary revenue is recorded
- [ ] Verify all payment processor deposits (Stripe, Square) match records

### Week 1: Reconciliation (Days 3-7)

- [ ] Reconcile Primary Operating Account
- [ ] Reconcile Business Savings Account
- [ ] Reconcile Tax Reserve Account
- [ ] Reconcile each Subsidiary Sub-Account
- [ ] Reconcile Payroll Account
- [ ] Reconcile Credit Card(s)
- [ ] Categorize all uncategorized transactions
- [ ] Assign proper subsidiary class to every transaction

### Week 1-2: Revenue & Billing (Days 5-8)

- [ ] Calculate management fees for each subsidiary
  - Subsidiary A: Revenue $_____ x ___% = $_____
  - Subsidiary B: Revenue $_____ x ___% = $_____
  - Subsidiary C: Revenue $_____ x ___% = $_____
- [ ] Generate and send management fee invoices
- [ ] Record management fee revenue in QuickBooks
- [ ] Send any outstanding customer invoices
- [ ] Follow up on past-due accounts receivable (30+ days)

### Week 2: Expense Review (Days 6-8)

- [ ] Review all expenses for proper categorization
- [ ] Verify no personal expenses in business accounts
- [ ] Allocate shared expenses to proper subsidiaries
- [ ] Process accounts payable (pay outstanding vendor bills)
- [ ] Review recurring subscriptions -- cancel any unused

### Week 2: Reporting (Days 8-10)

- [ ] Generate Profit & Loss (P&L) per subsidiary (by Class)
- [ ] Generate Consolidated P&L (all entities combined)
- [ ] Generate Balance Sheet
- [ ] Generate Cash Flow Statement
- [ ] Generate Accounts Receivable Aging Report
- [ ] Generate Accounts Payable Aging Report
- [ ] Update cash flow forecast for next 3 months
- [ ] File all reports in Google Drive: `Financial/[Year]/[Month]/`

### Week 2: Tax & Payroll (Days 8-10)

- [ ] Verify payroll was processed correctly
- [ ] Confirm payroll taxes deposited on time
- [ ] If quarterly payment month (Mar, Jun, Sep, Dec): calculate and submit estimated tax payment via EFTPS
- [ ] Update tax reserve account balance
- [ ] Set aside 25-30% of net profit in tax reserve

### Week 2: Review & Planning (Day 10)

- [ ] Compare actual vs. budget for each subsidiary
- [ ] Note any significant variances (>10% over/under budget)
- [ ] Update annual forecast based on actuals
- [ ] Identify any cash flow concerns for next month
- [ ] Prepare summary for monthly subsidiary performance reviews
- [ ] Send financial summary to Managing Member
