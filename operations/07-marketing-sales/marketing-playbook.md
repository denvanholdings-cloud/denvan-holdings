# Marketing Playbook

## Denvan Holdings LLC - Marketing Strategy

---

## Brand Voice

### Tone: Professional, confident, approachable
- **Do:** Sound knowledgeable, strategic, and trustworthy
- **Do:** Use clear, direct language without jargon
- **Do:** Be confident about our capabilities
- **Don't:** Sound corporate and stuffy
- **Don't:** Overpromise or use hype language
- **Don't:** Be informal or use slang

### Key Messages
1. "Building Businesses. Creating Legacy."
2. We build, manage, and grow diversified businesses under one corporate umbrella
3. Enterprise-grade resources for growing businesses
4. Shared services that reduce costs and accelerate growth
5. Strategic management across multiple industries

## SEO Strategy

### Target Keywords
- Holding company Austin Texas
- Multi-industry holding company
- Business management services
- Shared services for small businesses
- DBA subsidiary management
- Diversified business portfolio
- Corporate parent company services

### On-Page SEO Checklist (per page)
- [ ] Unique title tag (under 60 characters)
- [ ] Meta description (under 160 characters)
- [ ] H1 tag with primary keyword
- [ ] Internal links to other pages
- [ ] Image alt tags with descriptions
- [ ] Fast page load speed
- [ ] Mobile-friendly design

### Content SEO (Blog)
- Publish 2-4 blog posts per month
- Target long-tail keywords
- Minimum 800 words per post
- Include internal links to service/about pages
- Add a CTA at the end of every post

## Email Marketing

### Email List Building
- Newsletter signup on website (already built)
- Offer lead magnet: "Free Guide: How Holding Companies Work"
- Collect emails from contact form inquiries
- Add new partners and contacts (with permission)

### Email Types
| Type | Frequency | Content |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Monthly Newsletter | 1x/month | Company updates, insights, featured blog posts |
| New Subsidiary Announcement | As needed | Introduction, services, how to get in touch |
| Blog Digest | 2x/month | Latest blog posts roundup |
| Partnership Outreach | As needed | Cold/warm outreach to potential partners |

### Email Platform
- Recommended: Mailchimp (free up to 500 contacts) or ConvertKit
- Set up welcome automation sequence:
  1. Welcome email (immediate)
  2. "About Denvan Holdings" (Day 3)
  3. Featured blog post (Day 7)
  4. Services overview (Day 14)

## Paid Advertising (When Ready)

### Google Ads
- **Budget:** Start with $500-1,000/month
- **Campaign type:** Search ads targeting high-intent keywords
- **Focus:** "Holding company services," "business management Austin"
- **Landing page:** Contact page or dedicated landing page

### LinkedIn Ads
- **Budget:** Start with $500/month
- **Campaign type:** Sponsored content targeting business owners, entrepreneurs
- **Audience:** Small business owners, CEOs, entrepreneurs in Texas
- **Content:** Thought leadership posts, lead generation forms
