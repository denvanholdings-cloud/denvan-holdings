# Company Formation Checklist

## Denvan Holdings LLC - Texas LLC Formation

---

### Phase 1: Legal Formation (Week 1)

- [ ] **Form the LLC with Texas Secretary of State**
  - File Certificate of Formation (Form 205) online at [sos.state.tx.us](https://sos.state.tx.us)
  - Filing fee: **$300**
  - Processing time: 2-5 business days (online), 5-7 days (mail)
  - You'll receive a Certificate of Formation with a file number
  - Choose: Member-managed LLC (recommended for single-member)

- [ ] **Designate a Registered Agent**
  - Must have a physical Texas address (not PO Box)
  - Options: yourself, or a service ($100-300/year)
  - Recommended services: Northwest Registered Agent, Incfile, LegalZoom
  - Agent receives legal/tax documents on your behalf

- [ ] **Apply for EIN (Employer Identification Number)**
  - Apply free at [irs.gov](https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online)
  - Form SS-4, online application
  - Receive EIN immediately (format: XX-XXXXXXX)
  - Required for: banking, taxes, hiring, opening accounts

- [ ] **Draft and Sign Operating Agreement**
  - Not required by Texas law, but essential in practice
  - See `operating-agreement-template.md` in this folder
  - Defines: ownership, management, profit distribution, dissolution
  - Keep signed copy with company records

### Phase 2: Banking & Finance (Week 1-2)

- [ ] **Open Business Bank Account**
  - See `banking-setup-guide.md` for full details
  - Required documents: Certificate of Formation, EIN letter, Operating Agreement, government ID
  - Recommended: Chase Business, Bank of America, or local Austin credit union
  - Open at minimum: Business Checking + Business Savings

- [ ] **Set Up Accounting Software**
  - Recommended: QuickBooks Online ($30/month) or Wave (free)
  - Configure chart of accounts (see `04-financial-operations/chart-of-accounts.md`)
  - Connect bank accounts for automatic transaction import
  - Set up classes/categories for each subsidiary

- [ ] **Get a Business Credit Card**
  - Apply after EIN is established
  - Recommended: Chase Ink Business, American Express Business
  - Set spending limits and track by subsidiary
  - Keep business and personal expenses 100% separate

### Phase 3: Compliance & Insurance (Week 2-3)

- [ ] **Register for Texas Franchise Tax**
  - Texas has no income tax but has a **franchise (margin) tax**
  - Register with Texas Comptroller at [comptroller.texas.gov](https://comptroller.texas.gov)
  - First report due May 15 following the year of formation
  - No tax due if revenue is under $2.47M (2024 threshold)

- [ ] **Obtain Business Insurance**
  - See `insurance-requirements.md` for full details
  - At minimum: General Liability ($500K-$1M coverage)
  - Get quotes from: State Farm, Hiscox, Next Insurance, Hartford

- [ ] **Check Local Business Permits**
  - Austin may require a general business permit depending on activities
  - Check: [austintexas.gov/department/business-licenses-permits](https://www.austintexas.gov/department/business-licenses-permits)
  - Home-based business permit if operating from home: ~$50/year

### Phase 4: Professional Setup (Week 2-3)

- [ ] **Set Up Business Email**
  - Google Workspace ($7/user/month) - info@denvanholdings.com
  - Set up DNS records: MX, SPF, DKIM, DMARC
  - Create email addresses: info@, admin@, accounting@

- [ ] **Set Up Business Phone**
  - Options: Google Voice (free), Grasshopper ($26/mo), OpenPhone ($15/mo)
  - Get a dedicated business number
  - Set up voicemail with professional greeting
  - Current number: (202) 415-9373

- [ ] **Create Business Address**
  - Physical: 5900 Balcones Drive, STE 100, Austin, TX 78731
  - Virtual office / registered agent address if needed
  - Register with USPS for mail forwarding if applicable

- [ ] **Build Website**
  - Deployed at: denvanholdings-cloud.github.io/denvan-holdings
  - Next.js on GitHub Pages (free hosting)
  - Pages: Home, About, Services, Insights, Contact, Investors

### Phase 5: Operational Foundation (Week 3-4)

- [ ] **Set Up Document Management**
  - Google Drive or Dropbox Business
  - Folder structure: Legal / Financial / Subsidiaries / Marketing / HR
  - Store: formation docs, agreements, tax returns, insurance policies

- [ ] **Create Company Records Book**
  - Certificate of Formation
  - Operating Agreement (signed)
  - EIN confirmation letter
  - Meeting minutes / resolutions
  - DBA registrations for each subsidiary

- [ ] **Establish Shared Services Framework**
  - Define which services parent provides to subsidiaries
  - Set management fee structure (10-15% of subsidiary revenue)
  - Create management agreement template (see `03-subsidiary-management/`)
  - Document SOP for each shared service

- [ ] **Plan First Subsidiary**
  - Use evaluation scorecard (see `03-subsidiary-management/subsidiary-evaluation-scorecard.md`)
  - Register DBA (see `05-legal-templates/dba-registration-guide.md`)
  - Follow launch checklist (see `03-subsidiary-management/new-subsidiary-launch-checklist.md`)
