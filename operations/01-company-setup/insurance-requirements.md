# Insurance Requirements

## Denvan Holdings LLC - Required Coverage

---

## Essential Policies

### 1. General Liability Insurance
- **What it covers:** Bodily injury, property damage, personal injury claims against the business
- **Recommended coverage:** $1,000,000 per occurrence / $2,000,000 aggregate
- **Estimated cost:** $400-$800/year
- **Required by:** Most landlords, clients, and contracts
- **Providers:** Hiscox, Next Insurance, State Farm, Hartford

### 2. Professional Liability (Errors & Omissions)
- **What it covers:** Claims of negligence, mistakes, or failure to deliver professional services
- **Recommended coverage:** $1,000,000 per claim / $2,000,000 aggregate
- **Estimated cost:** $500-$1,500/year
- **Important for:** Consulting, management services, advisory work
- **Providers:** Hiscox, CoverWallet, Embroker

### 3. Business Owner's Policy (BOP)
- **What it covers:** Bundles general liability + commercial property + business interruption
- **Recommended coverage:** $1,000,000 liability / property at replacement value
- **Estimated cost:** $500-$1,200/year
- **Best for:** Combines multiple coverages at a discount
- **Providers:** Next Insurance, Hartford, State Farm

### 4. Cyber Liability Insurance
- **What it covers:** Data breaches, cyberattacks, ransomware, client data exposure
- **Recommended coverage:** $500,000-$1,000,000
- **Estimated cost:** $500-$1,000/year
- **Important for:** Any business handling customer/client data digitally
- **Providers:** Coalition, Corvus, Hiscox

## Additional Policies (As Needed)

### 5. Directors & Officers (D&O) Insurance
- **What it covers:** Claims against company leaders for management decisions
- **When needed:** When you have investors, board members, or advisory board
- **Estimated cost:** $1,000-$3,000/year
- **Providers:** Embroker, Vouch, CoverWallet

### 6. Workers' Compensation Insurance
- **What it covers:** Employee injuries and illnesses on the job
- **When needed:** When you hire employees (Texas does not require it, but strongly recommended)
- **Estimated cost:** $0.75-$2.50 per $100 of payroll
- **Providers:** State Farm, Hartford, Texas Mutual

### 7. Commercial Auto Insurance
- **What it covers:** Vehicles used for business purposes
- **When needed:** If any business vehicles are owned or leased
- **Estimated cost:** $1,200-$2,400/year per vehicle
- **Providers:** Progressive Commercial, State Farm, Geico Business

### 8. Umbrella Policy
- **What it covers:** Extra liability coverage above other policy limits
- **Recommended coverage:** $1,000,000-$5,000,000
- **When needed:** When total assets exceed primary policy limits
- **Estimated cost:** $500-$1,500/year
- **Providers:** Hartford, State Farm, Nationwide

## Action Steps

1. [ ] Get quotes from at least 3 providers
2. [ ] Start with General Liability + Professional Liability at minimum
3. [ ] Consider a BOP bundle for better rates
4. [ ] Add Cyber Liability if handling any customer data
5. [ ] Review coverage annually and after each new subsidiary launch
6. [ ] Keep certificates of insurance (COI) in company records
7. [ ] Add subsidiary activities to policies as you launch them
8. [ ] Request COIs from any contractors or vendors working for you
