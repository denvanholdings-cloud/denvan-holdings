# Revenue Streams

## How Denvan Holdings Generates Income

---

## Revenue Stream Summary

| Stream | Description | Typical % |
|--------|------------|-----------|
| Management Fees | % of subsidiary revenue for shared services | 10-15% |
| Profit Distributions | Net profit from subsidiary operations | Varies |
| Consulting Fees | Advisory services to subsidiaries or external clients | Project-based |
| Referral Fees | Cross-subsidiary customer referrals | 5-10% of deal |
| Licensing Fees | Subsidiaries using parent's systems/IP/brand | Flat fee |

---

## 1. Management Fees (Primary Revenue)

The core revenue stream. Each subsidiary pays a percentage of its gross revenue to the parent company in exchange for shared services.

**Standard Rate:** 10-15% of gross monthly revenue

### What's Included in the Management Fee:
- Financial management and bookkeeping
- Tax preparation and compliance
- Legal support and contract review
- HR and payroll administration
- IT infrastructure and support
- Marketing strategy and brand management
- Strategic planning and business advisory
- Performance monitoring and reporting

### Example Calculation

**Subsidiary A - Monthly Revenue: $40,000**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Gross Revenue | $40,000 |
| Management Fee (12%) | $4,800 |
| Subsidiary Operating Expenses | $22,000 |
| Subsidiary Net Profit | $13,200 |
| → To Parent (management fee) | $4,800 |
| → Retained by Subsidiary | $13,200 |

---

## 2. Profit Distributions

After subsidiaries pay their operating expenses and management fees, remaining profit flows to the parent company.

### Example: Portfolio of 3 Subsidiaries (Monthly)

| Subsidiary | Revenue | Mgmt Fee (12%) | Op. Expenses | Net Profit |
|-----------|---------|----------------|-------------|------------|
| Sub A | $40,000 | $4,800 | $22,000 | $13,200 |
| Sub B | $25,000 | $3,000 | $14,000 | $8,000 |
| Sub C | $60,000 | $7,200 | $35,000 | $17,800 |
| **Total** | **$125,000** | **$15,000** | **$71,000** | **$39,000** |

**Monthly income to parent company:**
- Management Fees: $15,000
- Profit Distributions: $39,000
- **Total: $54,000/month**

**Parent Company Expenses (shared services):**
- Accounting/Bookkeeping: $2,000
- Legal retainer: $1,500
- Marketing: $3,000
- Technology/IT: $1,000
- Insurance: $300
- Office/Admin: $1,500
- **Total: $9,300/month**

**Net to Owner: $54,000 - $9,300 = $44,700/month**

## 3. Consulting & Advisory Fees

The parent company can charge additional fees for specific strategic projects beyond standard shared services.

### Examples:
- Business plan development for a new subsidiary: $2,500-$5,000
- Market research and competitive analysis: $1,500-$3,000
- Operational restructuring: $5,000-$10,000
- M&A advisory (acquisition evaluation): $5,000-$15,000

### External Consulting
Denvan Holdings can also offer advisory services to businesses outside the portfolio:
- Holding company setup consulting
- Business structure advisory
- Shared services implementation
- Multi-entity management consulting

---

## 4. Cross-Subsidiary Referral Fees

When one subsidiary refers a customer to another subsidiary in the portfolio, a referral fee can be structured.

**Standard Rate:** 5-10% of the referred deal value

### Example:
- Subsidiary A (marketing agency) refers a client to Subsidiary B (web development)
- Subsidiary B closes a $10,000 project
- Subsidiary A earns a $500-$1,000 referral fee

This incentivizes subsidiaries to actively promote each other.

---

## 5. Licensing & System Fees

If the parent company develops proprietary systems, processes, or intellectual property, subsidiaries can be charged licensing fees.

### Examples:
- CRM system access: $200/month per subsidiary
- Proprietary training program: $500 per new employee
- Brand licensing (use of parent brand): included in management fee
- Software tools negotiated at bulk rates: pass-through at cost + markup

## Key Financial Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target | How to Calculate |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Portfolio Revenue | Growing monthly | Sum of all subsidiary revenue |
| Management Fee Collection Rate | 100% | Fees collected / fees billed |
| Parent Company Margin | 60%+ | Parent net income / parent revenue |
| Revenue per Subsidiary | $30K+/month | Individual subsidiary revenue |
| Subsidiary Profit Margin | 25-40% | Subsidiary profit / subsidiary revenue |
| Cash Reserve | 3-6 months expenses | Cash in savings / monthly expenses |
