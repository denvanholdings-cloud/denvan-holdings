# Shared Services Breakdown

## What Denvan Holdings Provides to Every Subsidiary

---

Each subsidiary pays a management fee (10-15% of gross revenue) that covers access to all shared services listed below. This replaces the need for each business to hire its own accounting, legal, HR, IT, and marketing staff.

---

## 1. Finance & Accounting

### What's Included
- Monthly bookkeeping and transaction categorization
- Accounts receivable (invoicing customers)
- Accounts payable (paying vendors)
- Bank account reconciliation
- Monthly P&L statement per subsidiary
- Quarterly financial review
- Annual tax preparation and filing
- Cash flow forecasting
- Budget creation and tracking
- Management fee calculation and invoicing

### Tools Used
- QuickBooks Online (accounting)
- Stripe / Square (payment processing)
- EFTPS (tax payments)
- Google Sheets (budgets, forecasts)

### How Subsidiaries Access
- All financial reports delivered by the 10th of each month
- Questions via email to accounting@denvanholdings.com
- Monthly 30-minute financial review call
- Real-time access to QuickBooks dashboard (view only)

---

## 2. Legal & Compliance

### What's Included
- DBA registration and renewal
- Business license management
- Contract review and drafting (standard agreements)
- NDA preparation
- Vendor/partner agreement review
- Regulatory compliance monitoring
- Intellectual property guidance
- Insurance policy management
- Annual compliance audit

### Tools Used
- DocuSign (electronic signatures)
- Google Drive (document storage)
- Legal templates library (in operations folder)

### How Subsidiaries Access
- Submit contract review requests via email to legal@denvanholdings.com
- Standard turnaround: 3-5 business days
- Urgent review available within 24 hours
- Complex matters referred to external attorney (billed separately)

---

## 3. Human Resources

### What's Included
- Job description writing
- Job posting to major boards
- Resume screening and initial interviews
- Offer letter preparation

- Payroll processing (bi-weekly or semi-monthly)
- Benefits administration
- Employee handbook and policy enforcement
- Performance review facilitation
- Termination processing and compliance

### Tools Used
- Gusto (payroll and benefits)
- Indeed / LinkedIn (recruiting)
- Google Forms (applications)
- Google Drive (employee records)

### How Subsidiaries Access
- Hiring requests submitted via standard form
- Payroll runs automatically on schedule
- HR questions via hr@denvanholdings.com
- Employee issues escalated to Managing Member as needed

## What's NOT Included (Billed Separately)

- External attorney fees for complex legal matters
- Specialized industry-specific compliance
- Large-scale advertising spend (media buying budget)
- Custom software development
- Physical office space and utilities for subsidiaries
- Industry-specific licenses and certifications
- External audit or forensic accounting
